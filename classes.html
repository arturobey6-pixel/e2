Knife Skills 101

The single highest-leverage class we teach. Master the pinch grip, the rocking motion and the five cuts behind 90% of home cooking — dice, mince, julienne, chiffonade, supreme. We finish with sharpening and care, because a sharp knife is a safe knife. You'll prep a full mise en place and take home the salad you built from it.

  • Pinch grip & knife motion fundamentals
  • The five core cuts, drilled hands-on
  • Sharpening & honing demonstration
  • Knife-buying advice (spoiler: you need one, not seven)
  • All knives & ingredients provided
